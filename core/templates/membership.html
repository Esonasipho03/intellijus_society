How to Complete Your Membership

  1. Download the Membership Form: Click the Download Form button or the image above to get the form on your device.
  2. Print the Form: Print the downloaded form on A4 paper for easy filling.
  3. Fill in Your Details: Complete all required fields carefully, including your full name, contact information, and student number.
  4. Read and Sign the Code of Conduct: Make sure to read the Code of Conduct included in the form and sign it to agree to the community rules.
  5. Submit the Form:
    • Hand-deliver the completed form to the IntelliJus Law Society office during working hours.
    • OR scan the completed and signed form and email it to membership@intellijuslaw.com.
  6. Pay the Membership Fee: Pay the annual fee of R20 at the office or via the payment methods mentioned on the form.
  7. Confirmation: After submitting the form and payment, you will receive a confirmation email with details about upcoming events and member benefits.

For any questions, please contact us at info@intellijuslaw.com.
